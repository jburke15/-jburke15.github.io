My Favorite Recipe - Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients Directions Nutrition Facts
4 slices white bread 3 tablespoons butter divided 2 slices Cheddar cheese Generously butter one side of a slice of bread. Place bread butter-side-down onto skillet bottom and add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side-up on top of sandwich. Grill until lightly browned and flip over; continue grilling until cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter and slice of cheese. 400 calories; protein 11.1g 22% DV; carbohydrates 25.7g 8% DV;fat 28.3g 44% DV; cholesterol 75.6mg 25% DV; sodium 639.2mg 26%
